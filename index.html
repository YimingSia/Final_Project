JUNE 23-27 | BERKELEY CA

BLUR MUSIC FESTIVAL

Berkeley's Premier New Wave Music Festival. In the heart of the San Francisco Bay Area, a new and exciting music festival is set to captivate audiences this summer. Blur, Berkeley's first-ever new wave music festival, is poised to bring the vibrant sounds of the 1980s back to life.

Curated by a team of passionate music enthusiasts with deep roots in the local community, Blur aims to celebrate the enduring legacy of new wave, post-punk, and alternative rock, showcasing both legendary acts and up-and-coming talent over the course of three sun-soaked days in the historic setting of Downtown Berkeley.
